Bart's Plan

Through this website, my objective is to create a personal portfolio that showcases my creative work, skills, and potential, while also continually updating my audience on my body of work as my portfolio expands. This website is aimed at industry peers, potential collaborators, employers, and clients who are interested in my work or future projects. The tone will be professional yet approachable, reflecting creativity and innovation, yet also simple and inviting. Visually, the colour schemes and formatting will be used to aid and enhance the artworks on display.

Portfolio Structure

Minimalist Design

Clean, spacious layout with minimal text, allowing the work to stand out. It’s visually focused, with large images and white space. The minimalist layout emphasizes the visual content, which helps guide attention to the projects themselves. I want to incorporate a similar minimalist structure for my own portfolio.

Smooth Scroll and Transitions

Smooth scrolling effects and subtle transitions when switching between sections or projects. The smooth transitions create a fluid browsing experience, something I would like to replicate to enhance user engagement.

Unique Personal Branding

Custom typography and creative use of a name/logo throughout the site. The unique branding is memorable and helps set the designer apart. I plan to create a personalized logo that reinforces my own brand identity.

Best Aspects

The homepage of the website features subtle, smooth background animations that create a dynamic and interactive experience without overwhelming the user. Moreover, each project in the portfolio grid features hover effects, adding an interactive element that reveals more information, luring the viewer to click on each project and learn more.

The colour palette primarily uses monochromatic tones with splashes of vibrant colour to draw attention to important elements, ensuring a clean and visually interesting layout.

The typography is bold and clean, utilising large, sans-serif fonts for readability and a modern, professional feel. The combination of minimalism, strong contrast, and thoughtful use of typography makes the design easy to navigate and user-friendly, which I find inspiring for my own portfolio, particularly in how it balances simplicity with impactful visuals.
Website Sitemap Overview

The sitemap for this portfolio website is structured to provide easy navigation and clear labelling of the various content. The viewer is introduced with the homepage, followed by a portfolio section that is divided into three categories: Graphic Design/Photography, Film-Making, and Animation. Each category contains individual project pages to showcase specific works.

Additionally, there is an "About Me" page that highlights my biography and skills, a potential blog section for posting articles or updates, and a contact section featuring a form and links to social media platforms for easy communication.
